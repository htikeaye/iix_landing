Introducing the Women's Health Bond

The United States spends $3 trillion annually in healthcare - more than any other country in the world. Despite this, there remains critical gaps in access and delivery. Women, especially immigrants and women of color, face huge barriers that prevent them from receiving adequate healthcare. Our Women's health Bond is an innovative financial structure that overcomes the healthcare gap by improving healthcare for under-served women in the US. The Women's Health Bond does this by providing support to high-impact social enterprises and organizations that have innovative and sustainable approaches to healthcare products, services and delivery. The Women's Health Bond will create bold new public private partnerships that will harness the power of capital markets for social good. It will unlock $100 million of private sector capital to improve access and affordability of healthcare for 2.5 million social good. It will unlock $100 million of private sector capital to improve access and affordability of healthcare for 2.5 million underserved women in the United States.

For the first time ever, you can participate directly and help us to create a better future for millions of women. We are raising funds to create new partnerships, bringing together grant funding from foundations, private capital and crowdfunding from thousands of individuals who care about equitable access to healthcare.

How Will the Women's Health Bond Work

WHB, a Special Purpose Vehicle, will be structured to impact healthcare organisations in prevention, early detection and treatment.

WHB to raise funds from investors seeking financial & social returns and benefit 2.5 million women.

WHB to improve access and affordability and strengthen organisations across the healthcare continuum.

WHB to be listed on the Stock Exchange - a sustainable channel to imapct and improve women's health
